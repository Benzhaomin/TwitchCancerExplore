About

Live stats and leaderboards built by monitoring the top 100 Twitch channels chatrooms.

Cancer points are awarded based on emote count, emote to text ratio, message length (extra short or extra long), and more.

The TwitchCancer suite of tools is released under the GNU General Public License, allowing anybody to copy, distribute and/or modify them. The source code for this site can be found at TwitchCancerExplore.git while the code for server-side stuff is at TwitchCancer.git.

The TwitchCancer family has a close friend: Twitch No Cancerino: cure chat cancer in Firefox.
